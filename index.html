FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AyeFace.

General
What is AyeFace?+
AyeFace is a universal AI-powered checkout and loyalty platform. It enables merchants to accept all payment methods and automate loyalty programs via facial recognition — turning every checkout into a revenue intelligence opportunity.
Is there really a free plan?+
Yes — our Free plan includes universal checkout, all payment methods, and automatic loyalty profile creation at no cost. No credit card required.
Do I need special hardware?+
No. AyeFace works with any device that has a camera — tablets, phones, existing POS terminals, or self-checkout kiosks. No proprietary hardware purchase required.
Which countries are supported?+
AyeFace operates across Southeast Asia with a primary focus on Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, and the Philippines. Cross-border payment capabilities are expanding to include China via TenPay (coming soon).
Payments & Integrations
What payment methods are supported?+
We support VISA, Mastercard, DuitNow, Touch 'n Go eWallet, GrabPay, ShopeePay, and many more across APAC. Our universal terminal accepts all major QR-based and card-based methods.
Can AyeFace integrate with my existing POS?+
Yes. AyeFace integrates with virtually any POS system via API. It can also operate as a standalone terminal without any POS, transforming any camera-equipped device into a smart checkout point.
How does the SEA to China cross-border payment work?+
Our upcoming cross-border feature allows customers from Southeast Asia to use their existing QR codes or cards at merchants in China via TenPay — one biometric confirmation, no manual currency conversion or extra apps. Available soon.
Privacy & Security
How is biometric data handled?+
Biometric enrollment is strictly opt-in. Facial templates are encrypted and processed in a privacy-preserving manner — raw images are never stored. We comply with GDPR and Malaysia's PDPA.
Is AyeFace GDPR and PDPA compliant?+
Yes. AyeFace is compliant with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). See our Privacy Policy and Security & Compliance pages for full details.
Can customers opt out of facial recognition?+
Absolutely. Facial recognition is always opt-in. Customers can pay using traditional methods at any time and can request deletion of their biometric data from the AyeFace User app or by contacting support.
Billing
How does billing work?+
Plans are billed per touchpoint (device) per month. Annual billing saves up to 33%. Add-on loyalty seats and AI passes are available at US$15/unit/month. No setup fees or hidden charges.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?+
Yes — we offer a 1-month free trial for our Growth and Enterprise plans. No credit card required to start. Apply here.
